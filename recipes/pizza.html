Odins World-Famous Pizza

To make this dish first you need to take pizza dough roll it bang ya sauce on and cheese and bake n eat.

Ingredients

  • dough
  • cheese
  • sauce

Steps

  1. make the dough and roll it
  2. add the sauce and cheese
  3. Bake at 180 degrees for 20 minutes
  4. Enjoy!
