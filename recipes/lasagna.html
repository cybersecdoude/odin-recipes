Odins World-Famous Lasagna

To make this dish first you need to take some pasta, boil it till al-dente, add ya sauce, add ya cheese, bake it, and voila its ready.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • cheese
  • sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Place in a pan and add the sauce and cheese in layers
  3. Bake at 180 degrees for 20 minutes
  4. Enjoy!
