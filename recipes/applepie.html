Odins World-Famous Apple Pie

To make this dish first you need to get the apples with some pastry bake em and gg

Ingredients

  • apples
  • pastry
  • baking tray

Steps

  1. boil the apples
  2. add to baking tray with pastry
  3. Bake at 180 degrees for 20 minutes
  4. Enjoy!
